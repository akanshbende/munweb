About Us

GECA MUN is an initiative of the students of Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad that works on the motto " Engage, Encourage, Empower ." It aims to create a platform to discuss, debate and work on the prevailing issues of national and international interests and represent the voice of the youth.

MUN was started as a method to bring the youth to professional discussions and give them a chance to put forth their views in a democratic method.
Following the steps, we at GECA MUN validate opinions and strive for action. Our value addition includes an overall personality development coupled with building a youth that can speak for themselves.
